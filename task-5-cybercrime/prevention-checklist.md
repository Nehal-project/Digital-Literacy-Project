# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
- Do not click on unknown links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
- Install apps only from official sources like Google Play Store.
- Regularly update your apps and phone software.

## UPI / Financial Safety (Important)
- Remember: You do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
- Never scan QR codes sent by strangers to receive payments.
- Always check payment requests carefully before approving.

## Social Awareness
- Do not overshare personal information on social media.
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)

## Final Advice
Stay alert, think before you act, and never rush financial decisions online.

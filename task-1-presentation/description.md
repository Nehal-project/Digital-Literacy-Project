### Design Summary:
I used Canva to create my digital literacy awareness infographic. Canva was helpful because it provides ready-made templates, icons, and design elements that made it easier to organize information in a visually appealing way.

My infographic presents a structured overview of digital literacy for students. It includes three main sections: understanding digital literacy, safe internet practices, and email etiquette for students.The first section explains how digital literacy involves accessing, evaluating, and creating information online. The second section highlights on online safety, including strong passwords, two-factor authentication, phishing awareness, and protecting personal information. The third section focuses on email etiquette like using clear subject lines, staying concise, and proofreading to ensure respectful, polished, and efficient workplace communication .

One thing I found interesting was how visual design can make information easier to understand compared to plain text. However, I found it slightly difficult to balance content and design, as adding too much text made the infographic look crowded. Overall, this task helped me improve both my understanding of digital literacy and my ability to present information effectively.
